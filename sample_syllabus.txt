Chapter 1: Introduction to Chemistry
This chapter covers the fundamentals of chemistry, including the scientific method, states of matter, and basic atomic structure. Students will learn about elements, atoms, molecules, and how they interact.

Chapter 2: Atomic Structure and Bonding
Explore the structure of atoms, electron configurations, and the periodic table. Understand ionic bonds, covalent bonds, and metallic bonds. Learn how bonding determines molecular properties.

Chapter 3: Stoichiometry and Reactions
Master the mole concept, chemical equations, and stoichiometric calculations. Learn about types of chemical reactions and how to predict products.

Chapter 4: Thermodynamics and Kinetics
Understand energy changes in reactions (enthalpy), spontaneity (entropy), and reaction rates. Explore factors affecting reaction speed and equilibrium.

Chapter 5: Acid-Base Chemistry
Study pH, buffers, and neutralization reactions. Learn about strong and weak acids and bases, and how to perform titrations.

Chapter 6: Oxidation and Reduction
Master oxidation states, redox reactions, and electrochemistry. Understand galvanic cells and electrolysis.

Chapter 7: Organic Chemistry Basics
Introduction to organic molecules, naming conventions, and functional groups. Explore alkanes, alkenes, and alkynes.

Chapter 8: Solutions and Colligative Properties
Study dissolution, solubility, and colligative properties. Learn about concentration units and how to calculate boiling point elevation and freezing point depression.
